Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how FitFindAI, Inc. ("FitFindAI," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile applications, websites, and other online products and services. Last updated: January 1, 2026. FitFindAI, Inc. is based in the United States.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information we obtain automatically when you use our services, and information from third parties. This includes:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Photos and images — photos you upload to search for clothing items. These are processed by our AI and are not stored beyond the current session unless you explicitly save them.
  • Usage data — search queries, items saved, pages viewed, and other interactions with our app.
  • Device information — device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and IP address.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including to:

  • Process and return fashion search results based on photos or text queries
  • Personalize recommendations based on your saved items and search history
  • Send you price drop alerts and notifications you have opted into
  • Communicate with you about your account, support requests, and product updates
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities to improve our services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party retailers and services to provide search results and pricing data. When you tap "Go to Store," you are redirected to a third-party retailer's website. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We may also use third-party analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics and Mixpanel to understand how users interact with our services.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, please contact us using the support form above or by emailing us directly. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
